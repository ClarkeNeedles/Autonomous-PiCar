## 1. Objective

To implement the Cabin and Restraint Equipment (CARE) system for passenger duck safety and enhance vehicle aesthetics with themed 3D-printed components.

## 2. Work Summary

### CARE System Design

**Lightning McQueen Passenger Cabin:**
- 3D printed Lightning McQueen body with integrated passenger compartment
- Interior cavity designed to accommodate duck passenger
- Restraint system planned using rubber bands or zip ties
  - Secures duck from the top
  - Allows easy removal and insertion of passenger

**Design Requirements Met:**
- Passenger containment during vehicle operation
- Quick access for loading/unloading
- Non-permanent restraint system (reusable)

### Aesthetic Enhancements

**Mack Truck Hat:**
- 3D model created for Mack truck-themed hat component
- Complements Lightning McQueen theming

## 3. Design Decisions

**Restraint Method Selection:**
- **Options considered**: Rubber bands vs. zip ties
- **Rationale**: Both provide top-down securing without permanent modification
- **Advantage**: Easy passenger exchange between runs
- **Final decision**: To be determined during physical testing

**3D Printing Approach:**
- Lightning McQueen body serves dual purpose: aesthetics + functional passenger cabin
- Integrated design reduces assembly complexity
