## 🐛 Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge 1: Audio Device Not Found

**Problem**: The Raspberry Pi failed to recognize the Robot HAT audio hardware. Running `aplay -l` returned "no soundcards found," preventing us from testing the text-to-speech features.
**Solution**: Deferred. We will focus on movement logic first and troubleshoot the I2S audio config next week, deemed not important right now.

### Challenge 2: Real-time Camera Preview via SSH

**Problem**: Tried to view the camera feed while connected via SSH, but couldn't.
**Analysis**: Sending raw video frames over the SSH encryption layer is inefficient.
**Solution**: We cannot use standard window previews (cv2.imshow). We need to implement a Flask web server to stream the video to a browser over the local network instead.
